Arsik

Arsik is a typical Batak Toba Samosir food, North Tapanuli, North Sumatra. Food made from goldfish which is seasoned with special spices from the mountainous region of North Sumatra, such as Andaliman, Kincung, Mobe, and other ingredients. The finely ground spices are smeared on the fish's body for a few moments, then cooked with water and a little oil, and low heat until slightly dry. Basically Arsik is like a yellow spiced fish, but with spices typical of North Sumatra. For the Batak people, this food is not just ordinary food, but also has its own philosophy and values. Arsik is usually served for certain traditional ceremonies. However, that does not mean that these foods cannot be consumed daily. We can enjoy the food and make it anytime.
