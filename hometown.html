Porsea

I live in Porsea. Small and peaceful place. Many views of the rice fields and the environment are still beautiful. For those who don't know, Porsea is a small town on the island of North Sumatra, close to Lake Toba. I can't show my house from google maps, because my house can't be reached by google maps. But my house is in this field area, the name of this area is "Tanah Lapang".
