Lake Toba

Lake Toba is located in the Bukit Barisan mountains, North Sumatra. It covers 1,145 square kilometers which makes it the largest lake in Southeast Asia. In the middle of Lake Toba there is an island called Samosir. The island has several villages with extraordinary natural attractions, such as mountains and waterfalls. If you take a trip to Lake Toba from April to August, you may be able to witness the traditional festivities and rituals of the Batak Tribe, namely the Lake Toba Community Party ceremony. The ceremony was held to remember Suka Batak ancestors by presenting Batak songs, sports events and dances. As for lodging on Lake Toba, you don't need to worry, because there are many ordinary resorts, hotels, and inns, each of which offers extraordinary views.
